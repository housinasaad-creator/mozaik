01

Cardboard Boxes (Folding Carton)

The everyday workhorse of retail packaging. Printed on quality folding boxboard, die-cut, creased and delivered flat to save space, then set up in seconds. Ideal for cosmetics, food, pharma and general retail — with any finish you need: matte or gloss lamination, foil, or embossing.

02

One-Point Side-Glued Folding Carton

A straight-line carton bonded along a single side seam — the most common tuck-end box. Fast to produce and economical, it suits light to medium products where a clean, reliable closure matters. A perfect canvas for full-colour branding.

03

Two-Point Side-Glued Folding Carton

A side-seam carton reinforced with two glue points for extra structural strength. The right choice for larger or heavier products that need a rigid body and a dependable seam that won't pop under load.

04

Crash-Lock Bottom Folding Carton

An auto-lock base that snaps into shape the moment the box is opened — no taping, no manual assembly. It carries real weight, making it ideal for e-commerce, bottles, jars and heavier retail items that need a strong, fast-to-pack bottom.

05

Four-Corner Glued Folding Carton

A tray-style carton glued at four corners to form a rigid open box. Popular for gift sets, produce, bakery and display trays where the contents are shown off and the structure must hold its shape.

06

Six-Corner Glued Folding Carton

A deeper, sturdier tray built with six-corner gluing. It holds heavier contents and stacks well — a favourite for produce, hardware and industrial display packaging.

07

Eight-Corner Glued Carton

A reinforced eight-corner box engineered for maximum rigidity and a premium, gift-grade presentation. Its double-wall corners resist crushing, making it a standout for luxury retail and heavier gift packaging.

08

Non-Glued Folding Carton

A carton that locks together with tabs and slots — no glue at all. Fully recyclable and easy to flat-pack and reuse, it is the sustainable choice for brands that want a clean, eco-conscious unboxing.

09

Slotter Containers

Standard slotted corrugated containers (RSC) — the backbone of shipping and transit packaging. Strong, stackable and cost-effective, built to protect products through the supply chain, with print options for branded shippers.

10

Telescope-Type Boxes

A two-piece box where a separate lid telescopes over the base — the classic rigid-box feel of premium electronics, apparel and gift packaging. Sturdy, reusable and unmistakably high-end, especially with foil, embossing or soft-touch finishes.

11

Self-Erecting Boxes

Pop-up cartons that spring open from flat into a ready box in one motion — a huge time-saver on high-volume packing lines. They cut labour and speed up fulfilment without sacrificing a clean, branded look.

12

Window-Patched Boxes

Cartons fitted with a clear film window so shoppers can see the product without opening the box — a proven driver of shelf appeal and trust. Perfect for food, toys, cosmetics and gifts where the product sells itself.

13

Spot UV Boxes

A finishing technique, not a shape: a selective high-gloss UV coating applied over a matte surface. The contrast makes logos and details catch the light and invites touch — an instant upgrade to premium perception at low cost.

Hot Foil Stamping

Metallic (or pigment) foil pressed onto the surface with a heated die — the timeless mark of luxury. Gold, silver, copper or holographic accents lift logos and typography, giving premium and gift packaging a genuinely crafted feel.
